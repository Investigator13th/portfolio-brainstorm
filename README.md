# 🎨 Portfolio Brainstorm

*Shape your personal homepage through natural dialogue — from concept to design direction in one flow.*

A conversational, one-question-at-a-time discovery process that helps you clarify what your portfolio site must communicate, who will read it, and what design choices best support your goals — before you write a single line of code.

---

### ✨ Core Highlights

-   **💬 Guided Conversation**: One question at a time, helping you refine your personal brand and presentation strategy step by step.
-   **🎯 Purpose-Driven**: Focuses on what a visitor should understand within the first 30 seconds, not decorative design.
-   **🛠️ Multi-Scenario Ready**: Works for job-search pages, internship portfolios, academic profiles, developer/designer showcases, and more.
-   **🧠 Clear Design Decisions**: From content structure to visual style, every choice is backed by a clear rationale.

---

### 🚀 Quick Start

> **"Please enable the Portfolio Brainstorm skill. I'm a recent master's graduate in AI product direction preparing for job hunting season. I need a personal homepage to showcase my project experience and blog — help me plan the structure."**
