# 🎨 Portfolio Brainstorm

*Sculpt the Perfect Personal Portfolio Before Writing a Single Line of Code.*

Building a great portfolio isn't just about code—it's about the story you tell. This skill guides you through a natural, engaging dialogue to define your audience, curate your best content, and establish a stunning visual direction, ensuring your website truly represents your brand.

---

### ✨ Core Features

-   **🎯 Audience-Centric Strategy:** Pinpoint exactly who you're targeting (HR, mentors, clients) to shape your information architecture.
-   **📑 Smart Content Curation:** Filter the noise. We help you extract the core highlights from your background and projects.
-   **🖼️ Stunning Visual Direction:** Explore visual languages ranging from sleek minimalism to cinematic vibes that fit your personal brand.
-   **🛣️ Guided, Stress-Free Process:** No overwhelming questionnaires. Just one thoughtful question at a time to build your blueprint.

---

### 🚀 Quick Start

Ready to brainstorm your next big personal site? Just tell the AI:

> **"Initialize the Portfolio Brainstorm skill. I want to build a new portfolio website, let's start the brainstorming process!"**
